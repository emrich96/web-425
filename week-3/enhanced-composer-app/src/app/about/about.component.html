Lin-Manuel Miranda

Best known for the musicals In the Heights and Hamilton, Lin-Manuel Miranda has become one of the most popular composers of the 21st century. He started composing and acting at a young age, writing the draft of his first Broadway musical during his sophomore year of college. Miranda has received multiple awards and nominations for his skills as a composer, playwright, and actor. Since starring in his major Broadway musical Hamilton>, he has gone on to write music for many major movies including Moana and Star Wars: The Force Awakens.
